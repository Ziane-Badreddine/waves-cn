## Features

- **Continuous speed slider** â€” smoothly adjust playback rate between `0.25x` and `4x` with fine-grained step control.
- **Preserve pitch** â€” toggle pitch preservation independently from speed so the audio stays natural at higher rates.
- **Live rate display** â€” a read-only badge shows the current playback rate as you drag the slider.
- **Drag to seek** â€” click or drag anywhere on the audio to jump to that position.
- **No re-render on speed change** â€” speed is applied imperatively via `setPlaybackRate`, so the audio is never destroyed or recreated when changing speed.
